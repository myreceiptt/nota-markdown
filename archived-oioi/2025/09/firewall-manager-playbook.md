## 5. SOP ‚Äî Communication Firewall

*(Confidential ‚Äî For Avatars Only)*

---

### **Objective**
To protect Prof. NOTA‚Äôs intellectual value, time, and energy from manipulative or unclear communications, ensuring that every interaction translates into **structured, fair, and compensated collaborations.**

---

### **Scope**
Applies to all incoming communications (chat, email, call, DM, in-person approach) initiated by external parties toward Prof. NOTA or his avatars.  

---

### **Principles**
1. **Polite but Firm** ‚Üí always acknowledge warmly, never rude.  
2. **No Free Substance** ‚Üí no idea, draft, or demo shared without agreement.  
3. **Redirect to Clarity** ‚Üí every vague message must be turned into scope, budget, and timeline.  
4. **Money First, Access Later** ‚Üí confirmed cash flow or contract before Prof. NOTA is engaged directly.  
5. **Firewall First** ‚Üí all contacts speak to the Firewall, not to Prof. NOTA directly.  

---

### **Standard Response Flow**

#### **Step 1 ‚Äî Initial Screening**
- If message is *friendly/social* (e.g. ‚Äúayo ngopi‚Äù, ‚Äúapa kabar‚Äù):  
  ‚Üí Respond with *Friendly-but-Firm* template.  
- If message is *requesting ideas/drafts* (e.g. ‚Äúbuat proof of concept dulu‚Äù):  
  ‚Üí Respond with *Redirection-to-Clarity* template.  
- If message is *potential but unclear opportunity*:  
  ‚Üí Respond with *Escalation-to-Contract* template.  

---

#### **Step 2 ‚Äî Clarification**
Always request:  
1. **Scope** ‚Üí What exactly is being asked?  
2. **Budget** ‚Üí How much is allocated?  
3. **Timeline** ‚Üí When is it needed and for how long?  
4. **Ownership/Contract** ‚Üí Who owns the output, and how is Prof. NOTA acknowledged?  

---

#### **Step 3 ‚Äî Positioning**
If unclear or no commitment shown:  
- Politely **decline casual meetups**.  
- Offer **consultation call (paid)** as entry point.  
- Stop further communication until they provide clarity.  

---

#### **Step 4 ‚Äî Escalation**
Only escalate to Prof. NOTA when:  
- Scope, budget, and timeline are confirmed.  
- Agreement or cash flow is already in motion.  
- The external party has demonstrated seriousness.  

---

#### **1. Friendly-but-Firm**
> Thank you for reaching out üôè.  
> At this stage, Prof. NOTA‚Äôs time is reserved for structured collaborations with clear value exchange.
